Staphon Arnold.Coloring outside the lines since 1988.

Staphon is a creative passionate about art, technology, and it's convergence. With a degree in Graphic design, a history in IT, and an early career in Brand and Identity, Staphon spends his time continuing to gather knowledge and resources for himself and those around him. Since 2017, Staphon has been employed at Pixar Animation Studios and is currently the Content and Training Associate for the Pixar University Filmaker Training team, where he helps support the platform used for documentation across the studio and contributes to learning enrichment around creative applications. Arnold also served as a co-leader of the African American/Black studio resource group for 4 years. Arnold currently resides in Martinez, CA with his wife and two daughters.
